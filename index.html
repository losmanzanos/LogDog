Who We Are

We're Chad and Miles, two guys who've been chums since the days of Trapper Keepers and Tamagotchis. After years of honing our carpentry skills, we decided to team up and share our passion for pergolas with the world.


At Log Dog, we don't just build pergolas - we unleash our inner barkitects to create custom masterpieces that make your backyard the envy of the neighborhood. We're not just smart, we're bark-smart. Our IB brains (Go Tigers!) let us tackle any design challenge you throw our way, from rustic to modern, and from small to large. We'll work with you to craft the pergola of your dreams, whether you want it to be a cozy retreat or a party hotspot.


So, if you're looking for top-notch timber works from two barkin' mad geniuses, give Log Dog a woof! We promise not to bite (unless you're made of wood).
